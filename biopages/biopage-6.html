Mariano Curiel

Director Expedition Operations

Arctic & Antarctic expeditions professional with a demonstrated history of experience leading polar expeditions and in the operational management field. Proficient in the design and delivery safe polar expeditions, organizational management, and team development.

Mariano has a wealth of polar experience from being the first Argentine to cross the Greenland Ice Cap unsupported to delivering and supporting numerous Antarctic expeditions as a guide to being a finalist on self-produced documentary film making at the BANFF Festival.

He has an incredible academic skillset including Antarctic Geology, Meteorology, operations in glacial terrain and Avalanche and Crevasse Rescue techniques, all of which will be highly beneficial to the expedition and its team members.

He is currently the Director of Expedition Operations.
